EMC Corporation Data Center Diagrams

Much of what I do for EMC bring clarity to complex concepts. This was a diagram revision I did for a client. The left shows the original "Big Animal Picture" for a Data Center. The right shows the revised version.

The process is fairly straightforward:

  1. Receive an original diagram, usually a Powerpoint or Visio file.
  2. Remove the information for each device I do not need. Everything besides model, serial number, raw storage, install date and expiry date get deleted.
  3. Replace the device images with a standard set created previously. All images are the same scale.
  4. Place the new images next to the originals.
  5. Delete the old images.
  6. Copy the necessary data from the old captions to the new captions.
  7. Align all similar devices along their top edge for each tier: server, SAN/network and storage/backup.
  8. Add connectors between devices corresponding to the type of connection in place: SAN, LAN/WAN, FICON or replication.
  9. Select all items and revise the font to the corporate standard.
  10. Repeat for each Data Center.
